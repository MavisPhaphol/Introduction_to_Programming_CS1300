Developing a School Talent Show

Script Writer and Backstage Crew, February 2020

I joined the team of script writers to write for the talent show, later when it's D-Day of the talent show, I became part of the backstage crew, setting the stage and making the props for different stages. We finished setting the show up by the time lunch ended, so for the rest of the day we have classmates, upperclassmen, and even freshmen coming in to watch the talent show.

While preparing for the script, we talk about the theme of the show and met with the hosts, performers, and many other people from the backstage and how we would like the show to run. We cut out props, painted them and adjust where the props would be hanging from the ceiling and decorated the stages with other painted props. It was a fun experience of being script writer and part of the backstage crew, meeting a lot of different people that are part of the show.
